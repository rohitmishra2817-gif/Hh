FAKE WIFE — 500 Episode Story (Hindi)

Contains episode summaries and an in-browser narrator (Hindi — male & female voice where available).
Episode 1 — Shaadi Ka Drama
Arjun, ek middle-class IT engineer, boss ki party mein jhoot bol deta hai ke uski shaadi ho chuki hai. Boss 'wife' se milna chahta hai; majboori mein Arjun ek unknown ladki Riya ko ek din ke liye fake wife banane ke liye convince karta hai. Party mein dono ki acting perfect lagti hai aur sab sach maan lete hain. Episode khatam hota hai jab Riya ko pehli baar Arjun ke ghar walon se milna padta hai.
Episode 2 — Ek Raat Ka Contract
Riya paiso ke liye fake wife banne ko ready hoti hai. Boss unhe honeymoon trip gift karta hai, jisse sabko aur yaqeen ho jata hai. Trip ke dauran chhoti-chhoti jhalakiyan dikhne lagti hain — dono ke beech ajeeb si chemistry. Lekin Riya ka ek raaz phone mein chhupa hua hai.
Episode 3 — Dil Ki Baat
Honeymoon trip ke chote palon mein Arjun ko ehsaas hota hai ki shayad yeh acting zyada gehri hai. Riya apne aap ko zyada khulkar behave nahi karti; uski nazar har waqt kisi dar se bhari rehti hai. Ek raat Arjun Riya se seedhi baat karne ki koshish karta hai, par Riya chup reh jaati hai.
Episode 4 — Past Ka Raaz
Riya ka ex-lover Rohit ki entry hoti hai jo use blackmail kar raha hota hai. Rohit ke pass Riya ke kuch aise saboot hote hain jisse uska poora jeevan khatre mein pad sakta hai. Arjun jab sach janne ki koshish karta hai to Riya aur door ho jaati hai. Family mein bhi pehli baar tanav shuru hota hai.
Episode 5 — Pyar Ya Drama
Fake relationship emotional war ban jata hai. Arjun Riya ko Rohit se bachata hai; dono ke beech ek emotional moment hota hai. Par Riya ab bhi apni purani zindagi chhupati hai. Audience aasha karta hai ki sach bahar aa jayega.
Episode 6 — Office Me Hungama
Arjun aur Riya ko office se 'Perfect Couple Award' milta hai aur unki photo viral ho jati hai. Social media par troll bhi aate hain. Riya ko sabke saamne apne role ko nibhane mein mushkil hoti hai; uska dil sach bolta hai. Arjun ka promotion bhi atka padta hai jab boss ko asli baat ka aasaar dikhne lagta hai.
Episode 7 — Blackmail Ka Khel
Rohit Riya ke kuch purane videos lekar blackmail karta hai. Arjun ke liye sab kuch ulta padta hai — uske career aur izzat dono pe sawal uthte hain. Par Arjun Riya ka saath chhodta nahi; dono milkar Rohit ka saamna karne ka plan banate hain.
Episode 8 — Real Love
Acting se zyada, dono ka rista gehra hota hai. Riya apni guilt confess karti hai aur bataati hai ki kaise uska past usse khinch raha tha. Arjun ka parivaar unse shaadi ke certificate maangta hai; ab jhoot bada banane ki zorurat padti hai.
Episode 9 — Sach Ka Dhamaka
Rohit sabko sach bata deta hai ke ye shaadi fake thi. Arjun ka boss gussa hota hai; job khatre mein padti hai. Arjun tab bhi Riya ke saath khada rehta hai. Par ek naya khatra nikalta hai: Arjun ka cousin Kabir, jise Riya ka behaviour pasand aa gaya tha, problem create karta hai.
Episode 10 — Nayi Shuruaat
Arjun propose karta hai: "Ab fake nahi, real wife bano." Riya ro padti hai aur haan keh deti hai. Par Kabir (Arjun ka cousin) silent observer rehta hai aur uski chuppi aage chal kar bada twist banne wali hai.
Episode 11 — Shaadi Ka Saboot
Arjun aur Riya official documents taiyar karte hain. Par Kabir secretely documents ko manipulate karta hai taaki Arjun par shaq ho. Riya ke past se judi ek aur aurat ka zikr hota hai jo Rohit ki saathi thi.
Episode 12 — Parivaar Ka Jazba
Arjun ki maa Riya par bharosa karne ki koshish karti hai par dadi ko kuch shak rehta hai. Riya apna past reveal karne ka plan banati hai lekin Rohit se dar lagta hai. Ek choti si party mein kahani aur tangled ho jati hai.
Episode 13 — Kabir Ka Iraada
Kabir Arjun ka promotion rokne ke liye kuch evidence plant karta hai. Riya is chori ko pakad leti hai, par isse Kabir aur zyada badal jaata hai. Kabir ka motive reveal hota hai: family property aur jealousy.
Episode 14 — Riya Ki Sacchai
Riya apne past ka ek bada hissa kholti hai: uski ek behen thi jo ek tragic incident mein gayi thi. Yeh sach Arjun ko hila deta hai. Rohit ki asli wajah samne aati hai — uska connection kisi political aadmi se hai.
Episode 15 — Public Scandal
Rohit Riya aur Arjun ki fake honeymoon ki tasveer social media par spread kar deta hai with malicious captions. Public opinion ulta hota hai; Arjun ki company me PR crisis. Dono milkar damage control karte hain.
Episode 16 — Dosti Ya Dhoka
Arjun ka ek puraana dost, Sameer, jo ab ek journalist hai, kuch hints dhoondta hai. Sameer Riya ki madad karta hai par khud bhi ek aise secret se jujh raha hota hai jo future me problem ban sakta hai.
Episode 17 — Ghar Ki Ladai
Kabir ne Arjun ke ghar me tension create kar di; dadi Riya ko ghar se nikalne ko kehti hai. Arjun dono taraf se phasa hua mehsoos karta hai. Riya ek chhota sa plan banati hai taaki Kabir expose ho jaye.
Episode 18 — Ek Nayi Dosti
Riya ka ek naya sahara milta hai — Meera, jo ek counselor hai, Riya ko support karti hai. Meera ke through Riya apne past trauma se ladna shuru karti hai. Rohit ki chaal aur complex hoti jaati hai.
Episode 19 — Khoj
Sameer aur Arjun milkar Rohit ki background investigate karte hain. Unhe pata chalta hai ki Rohit ek chhoti criminal gang ka hissa hai. Kabir ke motives aur bhi dark nazar aate hain — uski financial greed reveal hoti hai.
Episode 20 — Ek Plan
Riya aur Arjun Rohit ko phasane ka plan banate hain. Meera, Sameer aur Arjun ki maa is plan me madad karte hain. Par plan me ek unexpected leak hota hai aur Rohit bhag nikalta hai, lekin ek aur anzar (villain) ka pata chalta hai.
Episode 21 — Trust Test
Arjun ko company me ek bada assignment milta hai jisme uska integrity test hota hai. Kabir ek aur kadam aage badhkar Riya ke khilaaf evidence plant karta hai. Riya aur Arjun ke beech trust test intensify hota hai.
Episode 22 — Aankh Micholi
Riya Rohit ko dhundhne ke liye alag tarike apnati hai; kabhi disguise, kabhi sneaking. Arjun company ki pressure se jhel raha hota hai. Meera Riya ko emotionally strong banati hai.
Episode 23 — Dil Ka Faisla
Arjun ek moment me decide karta hai ki wo Riya ke sath sacchai ke liye ladhega. Uski job risk me padti hai lekin usne apne decisions ki zimmedari le li. Family ke kuch members bhi gradually sympathetic ho jate hain.
Episode 24 — Purane Dukh
Riya apni behen se judi ek diary dhundti hai jisme kuch important clues hote hain. Diary se pata chalta hai ki Riya ka past kisi bade political scandal se juda hai. Ab kahani national-level complexity lene lagti hai.
Episode 25 — Media Ka Daur
Sameer kuch exclusive clues publish karta hai. Media attention milta hai lekin saath me pressure bhi. Rohit ki gang aur zyada aggressive ho jati hai; Kabir apni nastiness ko aur expose karta hai.
Episode 26 — Raat Ka Sangharsh
Riya ek raat Rohit ke hideout me chali jaati hai taaki usse evidence mile. Action sequence hota hai jisme Riya barely bachi. Arjun ko pata chalta hai aur wo guilty feel karta hai kyunki usne Riya ko itna risk liya diya tha.
Episode 27 — Court Ka Chakkar
Riya aur Arjun ko court me bulaya jata hai jab Rohit ek false FIR file kar deta hai. Legal drama shuru hota hai; Sameer aur Meera key witness bante hain. Arjun ka career aur reputation dono pe sawal khade ho jate hain.
Episode 28 — Sacchai Ka Kshana
Court ke beech me ek chota sa witness sach bol deta hai jisse Rohit ki credibility girti hai. Lekin is revelation se naya conspiracy samne aata hai — kisi aur ne Rohit ko use kiya tha. Kabir ke kuch aur hidden moves reveal hote hain.
Episode 29 — Nayi Dushmani
Jo aadmi Rohit ko control kar raha tha, uska profile milta hai: ek local politician. Ab kahani me political danger add hota hai. Arjun aur Riya ko apni safety ke baare me sochna padta hai.
Episode 30 — Ek Naya Mor
Arjun Riya aur team ek naya plan banate hain taaki politician ke khilaf evidence jam ho. Par is mission ke dauran Meera kidnap ho jaati hai — stakes aur bhi badh jaate hain. Season 1 ke end par ek bade cliffhanger ka promise hota hai.
